How to use this website

The purpose of this website is to help you keep track of your learning routine and motivate you to study every single day, even when you only have as little as 15 minutes


  • Choose one (or more than one) activity to do based on how much time you have and how you feel that day.
  • Click on button with utensils to add task
  • Click on Trash button to delete task from todo list
  • Click on Check button to complete task from todo list

There is an auto changing mode, it will adapt to your current time
You can also change mode by clicking on Sun/Moon
